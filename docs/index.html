Our Story

Small hands.
Big artistry.

The Moon Penguin Shop was born from a love of beautiful, functional objects — the kind you reach for every day and feel a little spark of joy each time.

Every piece is made entirely by hand in Westport, Connecticut. Brass figurals are carefully set into hand-painted resin, creating one-of-a-kind accessories that are as practical as they are beautiful.

From pill boxes to compact mirrors, bag hooks to keychains — each item is a tiny work of art, made with intention and love.

The Process

Made entirely by hand,
every single time

01
Source & Select

Each brass figural is carefully chosen for its character and detail — animals, celestial motifs, cherubs, and more.

02
Pour & Cure

Richly pigmented resin is carefully poured and cured, locking in color and creating a flawless, glossy surface around each brass figural.

03

Each piece is set and polished by hand — the natural variation in resin means no two are exactly alike.

04

Every order is carefully packaged and shipped from Westport, CT with care and attention.
