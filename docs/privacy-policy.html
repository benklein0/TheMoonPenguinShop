Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Moon Penguin Shop ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our website at themoonpenguinshop.com and our use of third-party platform APIs, including the Pinterest API.

1. Who We Are

The Moon Penguin Shop is a small handmade accessories business based in Westport, Connecticut. We sell handcrafted brass and resin accessories through our Etsy shop at etsy.com/shop/TheMoonPenguinShop. This website serves as our company homepage and is used to promote our products.

2. Information We Collect

This website does not collect personal information from visitors. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any form of user data collection on this site.

We use the Pinterest API solely for the purpose of publishing our own product content to our own Pinterest business account. We do not collect, store, or process any data belonging to Pinterest users other than our own account.

3. How We Use Information

Our use of the Pinterest API is limited to:

  • Publishing product pins to our own Pinterest business account
  • Promoting our handmade products to potential customers

We do not use the Pinterest API to access, collect, or store any data from other Pinterest users. We do not perform analytics on Pinterest user data. We do not use Pinterest-derived data for advertising targeting.

4. Data Retention

We do not store any Pinterest-derived data. All API calls are made in real time to publish content, and no data is retained after the publishing action is complete. If a user disconnects Pinterest from our application, no residual data remains as none was stored to begin with.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, redistribute, or otherwise transfer any Pinterest content or Pinterest-derived data to third parties. We do not use Pinterest data in connection with any advertising network, data broker, or external service.

6. Pinterest API Disclosure

This application uses the Pinterest API to enable automated publishing of product content to our own Pinterest business account. The Moon Penguin Shop is an independent business and is not endorsed by, affiliated with, or sponsored by Pinterest, Inc. Pinterest is a trademark of Pinterest, Inc.

7. External Links

This website contains links to our Etsy shop and Instagram account. These are third-party platforms with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
